About GLAVID

We at GLAVID think in line with SpaceX for humans to live a multiplanetry life. According to NASA and SpaceX it is possible for a million humans to live in Mars by 2060, but what if we could shorten that? What if we could bring that down to 2035? We all know that Mars is a very dirty, dusty and cold planet, way more cold than Earth. This is where GLAVID comes into play. We speed up the journey of humans to Mars. We, in line with SpaceX are trying to clean off the debris in Mars and also purify the oxygen there. If you want to help us in thid mission, you could donate to our foundation , and maybe you could be one of the first humans to experience life in Mars.

The Problem

We all know that there are more than 7billion people living on Earth right now, and Earth is getting quite full. I mean by 2050, we should be seeing some changes in our Earth's atmosphere. This is why we should move/deivide ourselves into multiple planets. NASA and SpaceX are on this already but can they carry the weight alone? Mars is a huge planet (maybe not as huge as Earth) but it's equally big. Exploration and fixing of this planet could take quite a while.

Our Solution

GLAVID works hand in hand with SpaceX and NASA to speed up the journey to Mars by doing the little things- clearing off the dusts and debris left on the planet, helping SpaceX to research on ways to improve the oxygen capacity in order to accumulate at least a million humans by 2035.
